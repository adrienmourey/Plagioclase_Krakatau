


Mg_equilibration_times - folder contains files that summarise the equilibration times of each mesh for different sizes and temperatures. Crystal sizes have been scaled based on the length of the c axis from the centre of the crystal (named c half axis in file). Equilibration time is in years and is based on the centre of the crystal reaching 150 ppm (140 + 10 ppm).

Images of crystal shapes are shown. You can also open the .geo files in gmsh, which will allow you to rotate them. 

Other folders (e.g. an743_dat):

These contain inividual files that were output by each model. Each model is named based on the mesh, size (length from centre to edge along c axis and temperature). 

E.g. fsp656_mesh_500.0_T1100.0_3D_plag_output.csv - used mesh fsp656 which was 500 um from centre to edge along c and was run at 1100 C. 

These files contain the full compostional evolution of the centre of the crystal through time from th initial conditions to the equilibration condition of 140 ppm. Feel free to plot these up or use for defining a new equilibration condition.

Conditions of modelling are Xan etc are in Krakatau conditions file.
